## üéØ **Industry Selection Framework**

### **üîç Choosing the Right Industry Specialization**

**Step 1: Identify Primary Industry**
- What sector does your organization operate in?
- What are the primary regulatory requirements?
- Who are your key stakeholders and customers?

**Step 2: Assess Secondary Industries**
- Does your project span multiple industries?
- Are there applicable cross-industry standards?
- What hybrid approaches might be beneficial?

**Step 3: Evaluate Specialization Depth**
- How mature is your industry's PM practices?
- What level of customization is required?
- Are standard templates sufficient or do you need deep specialization?
