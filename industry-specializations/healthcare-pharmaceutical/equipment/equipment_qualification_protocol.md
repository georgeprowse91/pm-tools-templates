## 1. Introduction

### 1.1 Purpose
This protocol defines the requirements, procedures, and acceptance criteria for the qualification of [EQUIPMENT NAME], Model [MODEL NUMBER], Serial Number [SERIAL NUMBER] to be used in the [SPECIFIC PROCESS/AREA]. The qualification will demonstrate that the equipment is installed correctly, operates as intended, and performs consistently as per design specifications and applicable GMP requirements.

### 1.2 Scope
This protocol encompasses the Installation Qualification (IQ), Operational Qualification (OQ), and Performance Qualification (PQ) activities required to qualify the [EQUIPMENT NAME]. The protocol is designed to verify that the equipment meets all specified requirements and is suitable for its intended use in a GMP-regulated environment.

## 8. Deviation Management

### 8.1 Deviation Reporting
All deviations from this protocol, including test failures, unexpected events, or protocol modifications, shall be documented using the Deviation Form below and processed according to [COMPANY] SOP [SOP NUMBER] - Deviation Management.

### 8.2 Deviation Assessment
All deviations shall be assessed for their impact on equipment qualification status, product quality, and regulatory compliance. The assessment shall include:
- Description of the deviation
- Root cause analysis
- Impact assessment
- Corrective and preventive actions
- Resolution plan
- Re-testing requirements (if applicable)
