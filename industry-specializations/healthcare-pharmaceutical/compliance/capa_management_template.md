## 1. CAPA Overview

### Issue Description

Provide a detailed description of the issue or nonconformity that triggered the CAPA process. Include:

- Date and time the issue was identified
- Location/department where the issue occurred
- Products, processes, or systems affected
- How the issue was discovered (e.g., audit, complaint, routine monitoring)
- Initial containment actions taken, if applicable

Example:
> On 2023-06-15, during routine stability testing of Batch XYZ123 of Product A, an out-of-specification (OOS) result was observed for dissolution at the 12-month time point. The OOS result was confirmed through retesting. Initial review of manufacturing records did not identify any obvious deviations during production.
