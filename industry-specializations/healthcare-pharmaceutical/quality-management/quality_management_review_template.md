### Quality Objectives and Metrics

#### Quality Objectives Review
| Quality Objective | Target | Actual Performance | Status |
|-------------------|--------|-------------------|--------|
| Reduce batch rejection rate | <2.0% | 1.8% | Met |
| Improve right-first-time (RFT) manufacturing | >95% | 93.5% | Not Met |
| Complete all CAPAs within target timeframe | >90% | 88% | Not Met |
| Maintain training compliance | >98% | 99.2% | Met |
| Reduce customer complaints | <5 per million units | 4.2 per million units | Met |

#### Key Quality Metrics
| Metric | Current Period | Previous Period | Target | Trend |
|--------|---------------|----------------|--------|-------|
| Right-First-Time (RFT) | 93.5% | 91.2% | 95.0% | ↑ |
| Batch Rejection Rate | 1.8% | 2.1% | <2.0% | ↑ |
| Quality Events per Batch | 2.3 | 2.7 | <2.0 | ↑ |
| On-Time Batch Release | 96.8% | 94.5% | >95.0% | ↑ |
| Documentation Error Rate | 3.2% | 2.8% | <2.5% | ↓ |

**Analysis:**  
While most quality metrics show positive trends compared to the previous period, we still have not achieved our target for right-first-time manufacturing and documentation error rate has increased. The documentation errors are concentrated in the sterile manufacturing area and correlate with recent onboarding of new operators. The improvement in batch rejection rate and on-time batch release demonstrates that our batch review streamlining initiative has been effective.
