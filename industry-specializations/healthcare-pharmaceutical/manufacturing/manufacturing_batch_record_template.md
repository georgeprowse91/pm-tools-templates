### Appendix E: GMP Guidance for Batch Record Completion

#### General Guidelines:
1. **Data Recording:**
   - All entries must be made in indelible ink (blue or black)
   - All entries must be clear, legible, and contemporaneous
   - No blank spaces should be left - draw a line through any space not used
   - Never use correction fluid or completely obscure an entry

2. **Error Correction:**
   - Draw a single line through the error
   - Enter the correct information
   - Initial and date the correction
   - Provide reason for correction where needed

3. **Critical Information:**
   - All critical process parameters must be verified by a second person
   - All calculations must be independently checked and verified
   - Any deviation from specifications must be immediately reported and documented

4. **Electronic Records:** (if applicable)
   - Follow system access controls
   - Ensure audit trail functionality is active
   - Electronic signatures must comply with 21 CFR Part 11
   - Backup procedures must be followed
