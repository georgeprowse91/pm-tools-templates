## ðŸ“ˆ Success Metrics

### How to Know You Chose Well

**Good Template Selection Indicators:**
- [ ] Team adopted templates without resistance
- [ ] Stakeholders find reports useful and clear
- [ ] Templates save time rather than create work
- [ ] Information flows smoothly between templates
- [ ] Project outcomes improve measurably

**Warning Signs to Adjust:**
- [ ] Templates sit unused
- [ ] Team complains about complexity
- [ ] Stakeholders ask for different information
- [ ] Maintaining templates takes more time than project work
- [ ] Templates don't match your methodology

## ðŸ”„ Adaptation Guidelines

### Making Templates Work for You

#### Always Customize:
- [ ] Company branding and formatting
- [ ] Role titles and organizational structure
- [ ] Approval processes and sign-offs
- [ ] Communication preferences and tools
- [ ] Specific industry terminology

#### Consider Simplifying If:
- [ ] Team is new to PM processes
- [ ] Project is short-term or low-risk
- [ ] Organization prefers informal approaches
- [ ] Resources are limited

#### Consider Enhancing If:
- [ ] High-visibility or high-risk project
- [ ] Multiple stakeholder groups
- [ ] Regulatory or compliance requirements
- [ ] Complex technical integration needed
