Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Profile Analyzer

Last updated: August 4, 2025

Information We Collect

This application accesses only your basic LinkedIn profile information that you explicitly authorize, including:

  • Profile name and headline
  • Professional experience
  • Skills and endorsements
  • Email address (for authentication)

We use your LinkedIn profile data solely to:

  • Analyze your profile for optimization opportunities
  • Generate recommendations for profile improvement
  • Create reports for your personal use

Data Storage and Security

Your data is:

  • Not shared with third parties
  • Not stored on external servers
  • Used only for the duration of the analysis session

Your Rights

You can revoke access to this application at any time through your LinkedIn privacy settings.
