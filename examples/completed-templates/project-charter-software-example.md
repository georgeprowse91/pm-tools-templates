# Project Charter Example: Mobile Banking App Development

**Project Name:** SecureBank Mobile Banking Application  
**Project Manager:** Sarah Johnson  
**Date:** July 14, 2025  
**Version:** 1.0

---

## Executive Summary

SecureBank is developing a new mobile banking application to modernize customer experience and compete with fintech companies. This project will deliver a secure, user-friendly mobile app supporting core banking functions with advanced security features.

## Project Purpose & Justification

**Business Case:**
- Customer research shows 78% prefer mobile banking over branch visits
- Current mobile app has 2.3/5 star rating with frequent crashes
- Competitor analysis reveals we're lagging 18 months behind industry standards
- Projected ROI: 165% over 3 years with $2.8M revenue increase

**Strategic Alignment:**
- Supports company's Digital Transformation Initiative 2025
- Aligns with "Customer-First" strategic pillar
- Enables competitive positioning in mobile banking market

## Project Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Deliver secure mobile banking app** with 99.9% uptime
2. **Improve customer satisfaction** from 2.3/5 to 4.5/5 stars
3. **Increase mobile transaction volume** by 40% within 6 months
4. **Reduce support calls** by 25% through improved UX

### Success Criteria
- App store rating ≥ 4.5/5 stars
- Load time < 3 seconds for all major functions
- Zero critical security vulnerabilities
- 80% user adoption within 90 days of launch

## Scope Definition

### In Scope
- iOS and Android native applications
- Core banking features: account balance, transfers, payments
- Enhanced security: biometric authentication, fraud detection
- Customer support chat integration
- Multi-language support (English, Spanish, French)

### Out of Scope
- Investment/trading features (Future Phase 2)
- Business banking functionality
- Web application updates
- Legacy system replacement

## High-Level Requirements

### Functional Requirements
- **Account Management:** View balances, transaction history, statements
- **Money Movement:** Internal transfers, external payments, bill pay
- **Security:** Multi-factor authentication, transaction limits, fraud alerts
- **Customer Service:** In-app messaging, FAQ, contact options

### Non-Functional Requirements
- **Performance:** 99.9% uptime, <3 second response times
- **Security:** Bank-grade encryption, PCI DSS compliance
- **Usability:** Intuitive design, accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1)
- **Scalability:** Support 500K+ concurrent users

## Budget & Resource Allocation

### Budget Summary
- **Total Project Budget:** $1,850,000
- **Development Team:** $1,200,000 (65%)
- **Technology & Infrastructure:** $350,000 (19%)
- **Security & Compliance:** $200,000 (11%)
- **Contingency:** $100,000 (5%)

### Resource Requirements
- **Project Manager:** 1 FTE (12 months)
- **Development Team:** 8 developers (10 months)
- **UX/UI Designers:** 2 designers (6 months)
- **Security Specialists:** 2 specialists (8 months)
- **QA Engineers:** 3 testers (6 months)

## Risk Management

### High-Priority Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation Strategy |
|------|-------------|--------|-------------------|
| **Security Breach** | Low | High | Penetration testing, code reviews, security audits |
| **Regulatory Changes** | Medium | High | Regular compliance reviews, legal consultation |
| **Technical Integration Issues** | Medium | Medium | Proof of concept, incremental integration |
| **Resource Availability** | High | Medium | Cross-training, contractor backup plans |

## Communication Plan

### Regular Communications
- **Executive Updates:** Monthly to steering committee
- **Sprint Reviews:** Bi-weekly with product owner and stakeholders
- **Technical Sync:** Daily standups with development team
- **Customer Feedback:** Weekly user testing sessions

### Escalation Path
1. **Project Manager** → Daily operational decisions
2. **Product Owner** → Feature/scope decisions
3. **Executive Sponsor** → Budget/resource decisions
4. **Steering Committee** → Strategic decisions

## Project Assumptions

- Bank's core systems API will be stable and available
- Regulatory requirements will remain consistent
- Customer preferences align with market research
- Third-party vendors will meet delivery commitments
- Internal security team available for reviews

## Project Constraints

- **Budget:** Cannot exceed $1,850,000 total
- **Timeline:** Must launch before competitor's Q2 2026 release
- **Technology:** Must integrate with existing core banking system
- **Compliance:** Must meet all federal banking regulations
- **Resources:** Limited to current team plus 2 contractors
