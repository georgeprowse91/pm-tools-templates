# Resource and Capacity Plan

Plan to ensure the program has the right skills and capacity at the right time.

Contents
- Role demand by increment/quarter
- Supply and allocation across projects
- Hiring, onboarding, and vendor augmentation plan
- Constraints and contingency options

Guidance
- Traditional: baseline resource allocations at phase plans
- Agile-at-Scale: plan by PI and team velocity; maintain buffers
- Hybrid: blend capacity commitments with adaptive rebalancing
