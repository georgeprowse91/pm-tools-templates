# Program Management Plan (PMP)

Purpose: Define how the program will be managed to deliver intended benefits and outcomes.

Sections
- Program Overview and Vision
- Benefits Realization Strategy
- Governance and Decision Framework
- Scope and Boundaries
- Stakeholders and Roles
- Delivery Approach (Traditional/Agile/Hybrid)
- Roadmap and Milestones
- Resource and Capacity Plan
- Risk and Issue Management
- Quality Management
- Communications and Reporting
- Change and Dependency Management
- Measurement and KPIs

Usage
- Traditional: baseline and formally control changes
- Agile-at-Scale: timebox planning to increments with adaptive updates
- Hybrid: maintain baselines at phase gates; iterate plans within phases
