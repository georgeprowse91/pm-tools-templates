## üìñ Usage Instructions

### Preparation Guidelines
1. Gather all financial data and validate assumptions
2. Conduct stakeholder interviews for benefits identification
3. Research market conditions and competitive landscape
4. Validate technical feasibility and resource requirements

### Financial Analysis Tips
- Use conservative estimates for benefits and aggressive estimates for costs
- Include sensitivity analysis to show impact of assumption changes
- Validate discount rates and financial methodologies with finance team
- Consider both direct and indirect costs and benefits

### Review and Approval Process
1. Internal review with program team and subject matter experts
2. Finance team review of all financial analysis and assumptions
3. Stakeholder review and feedback incorporation
4. Executive presentation and approval request
