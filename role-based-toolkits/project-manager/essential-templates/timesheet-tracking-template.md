### How to Complete This Timesheet

1. **Fill in header information** at the top of the timesheet
2. **Record daily activities** with start/end times for each project or task
3. **Use project codes** provided by your manager or PMO
4. **Calculate total hours** for each day and each project
5. **Complete weekly summary** section
6. **Submit by [day of week]** each week
7. **Keep copies** for your records

### Time Tracking Tips

- **Track time in real-time** when possible
- **Be specific** in your task descriptions
- **Round to nearest quarter hour** (0.25, 0.50, 0.75)
- **Include breaks** of 30 minutes or more
- **Submit on time** to avoid payroll delays
