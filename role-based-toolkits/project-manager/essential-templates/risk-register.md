## Risk Categories

- **Organizational**: Skills, process, political

## Risk Assessment Criteria

### Probability Scale
- **High (H)**: 70-90% likelihood
- **Medium (M)**: 30-70% likelihood
- **Low (L)**: 5-30% likelihood

### Impact Scale
- **High (H)**: Major impact to project objectives
- **Medium (M)**: Moderate impact, manageable
- **Low (L)**: Minor impact, minimal effect

### Risk Score Matrix
| Impact/Probability | Low | Medium | High |
|-------------------|-----|--------|------|
| **High** | Medium | High | Critical |
| **Medium** | Low | Medium | High |
| **Low** | Low | Low | Medium |

## Risk Response Strategies

### Avoid
- Eliminate the risk by changing project approach
- Example: Change technology to avoid technical risk

### Mitigate
- Reduce probability or impact
- Example: Additional testing to reduce defect risk

### Transfer
- Shift risk to another party
- Example: Insurance, contracts, outsourcing

### Accept
- Acknowledge risk and monitor
- Example: Accept low-probability, low-impact risks
