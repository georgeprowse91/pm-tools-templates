## Communication Methods

### Meetings
- **Daily Standups**: 15 minutes, team status
- **Weekly Status**: 30 minutes, sponsor update
- **Monthly Reviews**: 60 minutes, formal review

### Documents
- **Status Reports**: Weekly written updates
- **Dashboards**: Visual progress tracking
- **Meeting Minutes**: Formal record keeping

### Digital Channels
- **Email**: Formal communications
- **Slack/Teams**: Informal updates
- **Project Portal**: Document repository

## Communication Guidelines

### Response Times
- **Urgent**: Within 2 hours
- **High**: Within 8 hours
- **Normal**: Within 24 hours
- **Low**: Within 48 hours

### Escalation Process
1. Team Level → Project Manager
2. Project Manager → Sponsor
3. Sponsor → Steering Committee
4. Steering Committee → Executive Level

### Meeting Standards
- Agendas distributed 24 hours prior
- Minutes distributed within 24 hours
- Action items tracked and followed up
