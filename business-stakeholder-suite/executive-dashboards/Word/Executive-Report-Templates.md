## ğŸ“ Word Template Features

### Document Formatting
- **Page Setup:** 8.5" x 11", 1" margins
- **Font:** Calibri 11pt body, Calibri Bold 14pt headings
- **Line Spacing:** 1.15 with 6pt spacing after paragraphs
- **Headers/Footers:** Company logo, page numbers, document classification

### Style Guidelines
- **Heading 1:** 16pt, Bold, Blue (Company color)
- **Heading 2:** 14pt, Bold, Black
- **Heading 3:** 12pt, Bold, Black
- **Body Text:** 11pt, Regular, Black
- **Emphasis:** Bold for key metrics, Italic for notes

### Table Formatting
- **Header Row:** Bold, shaded background
- **Alternating Rows:** Light gray shading
- **Borders:** Thin black lines
- **Currency:** Right-aligned, $ format
- **Percentages:** Right-aligned, % format

### Professional Elements
- **Page Breaks:** Between major sections
- **Watermarks:** "CONFIDENTIAL" or "INTERNAL USE"
- **Document Properties:** Auto-populated metadata
- **Table of Contents:** Auto-generated with page numbers
- **Cross-References:** Automatic section/table references
