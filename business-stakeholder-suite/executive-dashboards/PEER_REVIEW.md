## üîÑ Review Process

### Stage 1: Individual Reviews (5 business days)
Each reviewer completes their assessment independently and documents findings.

### Stage 2: Collaborative Review (2 business days)  
Team meeting to discuss findings, resolve conflicts, and align on recommendations.

### Stage 3: Issue Resolution (3 business days)
Address identified issues and re-review changed components.

### Stage 4: Final Sign-off (1 business day)
All reviewers provide final approval or escalate remaining concerns.
