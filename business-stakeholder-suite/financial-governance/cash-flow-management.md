### 2. Cash Flow Forecasting

#### Forecasting Methods
```
Direct Method:
- Detailed cash receipts and payments
- Line-by-line analysis
- High accuracy for short-term

- Adjust for non-cash items
- Working capital changes
- Suitable for longer-term

Rolling Forecast:
- 13-week rolling basis
- Updated weekly/monthly
- Continuous refinement
- Dynamic scenario planning
```

### 3. Cash Flow Monitoring

#### Key Performance Indicators
```
Liquidity Ratios:
- Current Ratio: Current Assets / Current Liabilities
- Quick Ratio: (Current Assets - Inventory) / Current Liabilities
- Cash Ratio: Cash & Equivalents / Current Liabilities
- Operating Cash Flow Ratio: Operating Cash Flow / Current Liabilities

Efficiency Metrics:
- Days Sales Outstanding (DSO): (A/R / Revenue) × 365
- Days Payable Outstanding (DPO): (A/P / COGS) × 365
- Cash Conversion Cycle: DSO + DIO - DPO
- Cash Flow Coverage: Operating Cash Flow / Total Debt

Profitability Indicators:
- Operating Cash Flow Margin: OCF / Revenue
- Free Cash Flow: OCF - Capital Expenditures
- Cash Flow ROI: (Cash Flow - Investment) / Investment
```
