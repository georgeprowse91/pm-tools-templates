## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### High-Risk Items
1. **Scope Creep Risk** - Epic has been open 85 days, indicating potential complexity issues
   - *Mitigation:* Phased delivery with clear scope boundaries and stakeholder sign-offs

2. **Integration Complexity** - Connecting 6 major components across 3 methodologies
   - *Mitigation:* Incremental integration testing and modular design approach

3. **User Adoption** - Complex suite may overwhelm users
   - *Mitigation:* Progressive disclosure, guided wizards, and role-specific entry points

### Medium-Risk Items
1. **Resource Dependencies** - Requires coordination across multiple domains
2. **Quality Assurance** - Large template volume requires systematic validation
3. **Documentation Debt** - Existing gaps may compound during rapid development
