2. **Analysis**: Identify patterns and root causes
3. **Documentation**: Capture lessons in project knowledge base
4. **Application**: Implement improvements in subsequent phases
5. **Sharing**: Communicate lessons to other projects

- Quarterly assessment of change management effectiveness
- Adjustment of strategies based on lessons learned
- Integration of best practices from industry and organization
