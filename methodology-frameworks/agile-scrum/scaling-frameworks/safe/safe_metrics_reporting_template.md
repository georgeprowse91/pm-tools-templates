### Appendix A: Metric Definitions and Calculations

#### Portfolio Level Metrics
**Epic Lead Time:**
- **Definition:** Time from epic entering funnel to completion
- **Calculation:** Done Date - Funnel Entry Date
- **Target:** <20 weeks for standard epics

**Portfolio ROI:**
- **Definition:** Return on investment across all portfolio initiatives
- **Calculation:** (Benefits - Investment) / Investment Ã— 100
- **Target:** >150% annually

#### Program Level Metrics
**Program Predictability:**
- **Definition:** Percentage of PI objectives achieved vs. planned
- **Calculation:** (Actual Business Value / Planned Business Value) Ã— 100
- **Target:** >80% consistently

#### Team Level Metrics
**Team Velocity:**
- **Definition:** Average story points completed per iteration
- **Calculation:** Sum of completed story points / Number of iterations
- **Target:** Consistent with team capacity, not increasing velocity
