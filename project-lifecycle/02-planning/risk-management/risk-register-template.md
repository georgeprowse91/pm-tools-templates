## Risk Categories
- **Technical Risks**: Technology, integration, performance
- **Business Risks**: Requirements, stakeholder, funding
- **External Risks**: Regulatory, market, vendor
- **Project Risks**: Schedule, resource, scope

## Risk Assessment Scale
### Probability
- **High (H)**: 70-90% chance
- **Medium (M)**: 30-70% chance  
- **Low (L)**: 5-30% chance

### Impact
- **High (H)**: Major impact to scope, schedule, budget
- **Medium (M)**: Moderate impact, manageable
- **Low (L)**: Minor impact, minimal effect
