## 1. Skills Matrix Overview

### 1.1 Purpose
The Skills Matrix provides a comprehensive view of team member capabilities, identifies skill gaps, and supports resource planning, training, and development decisions.

### 1.2 Scope
This matrix covers:
- Technical skills and competencies
- Business and domain knowledge
- Soft skills and leadership capabilities
- Certifications and qualifications
- Experience levels and expertise areas
- Learning and development needs

### 1.3 Benefits
- **Resource Planning**: Optimal assignment of team members to tasks
- **Risk Mitigation**: Identify and address skill gaps early
- **Career Development**: Support individual growth and development
- **Knowledge Management**: Prevent knowledge concentration risks
- **Team Building**: Build balanced, high-performing teams
