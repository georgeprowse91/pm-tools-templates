## Issue Categories
- **Technical Issues**: System problems, bugs, performance
- **Process Issues**: Workflow, procedure, methodology
- **Resource Issues**: People, equipment, budget
- **External Issues**: Vendor, regulatory, stakeholder

## Priority Levels
- **Critical**: Project stopping, immediate attention required
- **High**: Significant impact, resolve within 24-48 hours
- **Medium**: Moderate impact, resolve within 1 week
- **Low**: Minor impact, resolve as time permits

## Resolution Process
1. **Identification**: Issue raised and logged
2. **Assessment**: Impact and priority determined
3. **Assignment**: Owner and target date assigned
4. **Action**: Resolution activities undertaken
5. **Closure**: Issue resolved and verified
