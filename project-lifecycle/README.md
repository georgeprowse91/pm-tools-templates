## ðŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### Template Selection
1. **Start simple** - Choose 3-5 core templates for your first project
2. **Build experience** - Add more templates as you gain confidence
3. **Customize wisely** - Adapt templates but maintain core structure

### Phase Management
1. **Don't skip initiation** - Poor starts lead to project failure
2. **Invest in planning** - 1 hour of planning saves 10 hours of execution
3. **Monitor continuously** - Small course corrections prevent big problems
4. **Close properly** - Capture lessons for future success

### Cross-Methodology
1. **Mix and match** - Use the best templates from each methodology
2. **Scale appropriately** - More risky = more structure
3. **Stay flexible** - Adapt as you learn what works
