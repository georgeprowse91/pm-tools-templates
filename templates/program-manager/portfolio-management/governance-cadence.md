# Portfolio Governance Cadence

Cadence:
- Monthly Portfolio Review: reprioritize backlog, approve funding changes
- Quarterly Strategy Update: theme refresh, roadmap roll-up
- Risk Council (bi-weekly): aggregate top risks/dependencies across programs

Inputs:
- Portfolio Kanban metrics, benefits dashboard, risk aggregation

Outputs:
- Updated prioritization, funding decisions, risk actions, comms pack for executives
