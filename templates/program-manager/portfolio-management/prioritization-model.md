### Scoring Criteria

**Strategic Alignment (Weight: 30%)**
- **5**: Directly enables strategic objective with measurable outcomes
- **4**: Strongly supports strategic objective with clear benefits
- **3**: Moderately aligned with strategic objectives
- **2**: Indirectly supports strategic objectives
- **1**: Minimal alignment with strategic objectives

**Business Value (Weight: 25%)**
- **5**: Very high ROI/NPV (>20% IRR or >$5M NPV)
- **4**: High value (15-20% IRR or $2-5M NPV)
- **3**: Moderate value (10-15% IRR or $1-2M NPV)
- **2**: Low value (5-10% IRR or $500K-1M NPV)
- **1**: Minimal value (<5% IRR or <$500K NPV)

**Cost/Effort (Weight: 15%) - Inverted Scale**
- **5**: Very low cost/effort (<$500K, <6 months)
- **4**: Low cost/effort ($500K-1M, 6-12 months)
- **3**: Moderate cost/effort ($1-3M, 12-18 months)
- **2**: High cost/effort ($3-5M, 18-24 months)
- **1**: Very high cost/effort (>$5M, >24 months)

**Risk (Weight: 15%) - Inverted Scale**
- **5**: Very low risk (proven approach, low complexity)
- **4**: Low risk (some unknowns, manageable complexity)
- **3**: Moderate risk (several dependencies, medium complexity)
- **2**: High risk (many unknowns, high complexity)
- **1**: Very high risk (unproven approach, very high complexity)

**Customer Impact (Weight: 15%)**
- **5**: Significant positive impact on customer experience/satisfaction
- **4**: Notable improvement in customer experience
- **3**: Moderate customer benefit
- **2**: Minor customer impact
- **1**: Minimal or no direct customer benefit

### Decision Rules

**Approval Thresholds:**
- **Automatic Approval**: Priority Score > 4.0 and within allocated budget
- **Portfolio Review Required**: Priority Score 3.0-4.0 or exceeds budget by <20%
- **Executive Review Required**: Priority Score < 3.0 or exceeds budget by >20%

**Investment Constraints:**
- Maximum 30% of portfolio budget allocated to high-risk initiatives (Risk Score < 3)
- Minimum 60% of portfolio aligned to top strategic themes
- Maximum 25% of resources allocated to single program/initiative

## ðŸ“– Usage Instructions

### How to Use This Template

1. **Preparation:**
   - Define and weight strategic themes
   - Gather program/initiative business cases
   - Assemble cross-functional evaluation team

2. **Scoring Process:**
   - Rate each program against all criteria (1-5 scale)
   - Calculate weighted scores using provided formulas
   - Rank programs by total priority score

3. **Decision Making:**
   - Apply decision rules for approval/deferral
   - Balance portfolio across run/grow/transform categories
   - Document decisions and schedule regular reviews

### Customization Guidelines

- **Adjust weights** based on organizational priorities
- **Modify criteria** to match specific context
- **Update thresholds** based on portfolio capacity
- **Review quarterly** during governance cycles

**Note:** This model should be calibrated with 3-5 known initiatives and weights normalized to sum to 100%.
