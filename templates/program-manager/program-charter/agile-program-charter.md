### Agile Charter Principles
- **Living Document:** This charter evolves as we learn
- **Value Focus:** Decisions prioritize user and business value
- **Transparency:** Information is visible and accessible
- **Adaptation:** We inspect and adapt regularly
- **Collaboration:** Success requires active participation

### Charter Evolution
- Review and update at end of each PI
- Major changes require stakeholder agreement
- Document all changes and rationale
- Maintain version history

### Success Tips
- Keep vision clear and inspiring
- Make objectives measurable and achievable  
- Plan for learning and adaptation
- Focus on outcomes over outputs
- Maintain regular stakeholder engagement
