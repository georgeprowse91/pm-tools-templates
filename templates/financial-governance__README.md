## ðŸš¨ Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Financial Planning Errors
- **Unrealistic estimates** without historical validation
- **Insufficient contingency** planning for risk scenarios
- **Missing stakeholder** buy-in for financial commitments
- **Poor baseline management** and change control

### Governance Failures
- **Inconsistent reporting** and communication gaps
- **Delayed issue escalation** without early warning systems
- **Inadequate risk monitoring** and mitigation planning
- **Missing audit trails** and compliance documentation

### Value Realization Issues
- **Benefit measurement gaps** without clear metrics
- **Stakeholder accountability** unclear for value delivery
- **Optimization opportunities** missed during execution
- **Lessons learned** not captured for future projects
