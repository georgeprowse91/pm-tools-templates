# Quality Management

Templates and tools for ensuring project quality throughout the planning phase.

## Overview

Quality management during project planning establishes the foundation for successful project delivery by defining quality standards, processes, and metrics that will guide the project team.

## Templates Available

### Quality Planning Templates
- Quality Management Plan Template
- Quality Metrics and KPIs Definition
- Quality Standards Checklist
- Acceptance Criteria Templates

### Quality Assurance Tools
- Quality Review Checklists  
- Peer Review Templates
- Quality Gate Definitions
- Testing Strategy Templates

### Quality Control Processes
- Quality Inspection Forms
- Defect Tracking Templates
- Quality Audit Checklists
- Corrective Action Plans

## Methodology Integration

### Traditional Approach
- Follows Traditional Guide quality management knowledge area
- Emphasizes plan-driven quality assurance
- Structured quality control processes

### Agile Integration  
- Definition of Done templates
- User story acceptance criteria
- Sprint quality reviews
- Continuous improvement practices

### Hybrid Support
- Flexible quality gates
- Adaptive quality metrics
- Integration checkpoints

## Getting Started

1. Select appropriate quality planning templates
2. Customize quality standards for your project
3. Define quality metrics and acceptance criteria
4. Establish quality review processes
5. Set up quality monitoring and control
