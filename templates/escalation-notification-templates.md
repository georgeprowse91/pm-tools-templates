# Escalation and Notification Templates (Program)

Provide ready-to-use notification patterns for different severity levels.

Templates
- High-Severity Issue Escalation (Executive + Program)
- Cross-Project Dependency Slippage Alert
- Risk Threshold Exceeded Notice
- Decision Required Notification

Guidance
- Keep concise and action-oriented
- Include context, impact, ask, and next steps
- Align with escalation matrix
