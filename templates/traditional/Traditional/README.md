### **Risk Categories**
- **Technical Risks** - Technology challenges, integration issues
- **External Risks** - Regulatory changes, market conditions
- **Organizational Risks** - Resource availability, skill gaps
- **Project Management Risks** - Schedule delays, scope creep

### **Risk Response Strategies**
- **Avoid** - Eliminate threat by changing project approach
- **Transfer** - Shift risk to third party (insurance, contracts)
- **Mitigate** - Reduce probability or impact of risk
- **Accept** - Acknowledge risk and develop contingency plans
