# Program Risk Management

Program-level risk management templates to aggregate and manage risk across multiple projects.

## What's Included
- Program Risk Register
- Program Risk Management Plan
- Dependency and Risk Aggregation Matrix
- Escalation and Issue Resolution Process

## Guidance
- Traditional: Formal risk reviews at governance milestones; quantitative analysis where appropriate.
- Agile-at-Scale: Continuous risk ROAMing; integrate with PI and iteration reviews.
- Hybrid: Combine formal registers with iterative risk surfacing.
