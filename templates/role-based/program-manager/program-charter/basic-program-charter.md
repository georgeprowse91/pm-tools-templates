## ðŸ“– How to Use This Template

### Preparation
1. Gather key program information from stakeholders
2. Understand business objectives and constraints
3. Identify all component projects and their relationships

### Completion
1. Fill in all bracketed placeholders
2. Customize sections based on your program's needs
3. Review with stakeholders for accuracy and completeness

### Approval
1. Present to executive sponsor and key stakeholders
2. Incorporate any feedback or changes
3. Obtain formal signatures and approval
4. Distribute to all program team members

### Tips
- Keep descriptions clear and concise
- Use specific, measurable language for success criteria
- Validate assumptions with stakeholders
- Plan for regular charter reviews and updates
