## üìñ Usage Instructions

### How to Use This Template
1. **Preparation Phase**
   - Gather all necessary business information
   - Conduct stakeholder interviews
   - Review organizational standards and requirements

2. **Customization**
   - Replace all bracketed placeholders with actual information
   - Adapt sections to match organizational needs
   - Add or remove sections based on program complexity

3. **Review and Validation**
   - Review with key stakeholders
   - Validate financial projections and assumptions
   - Ensure alignment with organizational strategy

4. **Approval Process**
   - Present to steering committee or sponsors
   - Incorporate feedback and revisions
   - Obtain formal approval and signatures

### Tips for Success
- Keep the executive summary concise and compelling
- Use data and evidence to support business case
- Be realistic about constraints and risks
- Ensure stakeholder engagement throughout development
- Plan for regular review and updates as program evolves
