### Tips for Effective Status Reporting

1. **Be honest**: Report actual status, not what you wish it was
2. **Be concise**: Keep content brief and focused on what matters
3. **Highlight actions**: Clearly indicate what's being done about issues
4. **Include visuals**: Use colors and symbols to quickly convey status
5. **Be consistent**: Deliver reports on the same day each week/period
6. **Avoid surprises**: Escalate issues early rather than hiding them
7. **Focus on exceptions**: Spend more time on problem areas than what's going well
