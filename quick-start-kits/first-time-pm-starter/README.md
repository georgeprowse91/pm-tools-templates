## Getting Started (10 Minutes)

### Step 1: Project Charter (5 minutes)
1. Open `project-charter-simple.md`
2. Fill in the basic project information
3. Get stakeholder approval on the charter

### Step 2: Stakeholder Register (3 minutes)
1. List everyone who cares about your project
2. Note their expectations and communication preferences
3. Identify your key allies and potential blockers

### Step 3: Communication Plan (2 minutes)
1. Decide on meeting cadence
2. Set up status reporting schedule
3. Create team communication channels
